📖 D2C Scaler Operator Guide

This command center aggregates daily, granular ad performance data across platforms, models attribution rates, and guides scaling limits.

📋 Required Spreadsheet Schema (RAW_DATA tab)

Your Google Sheet or clipboard pastes must contain columns formatted exactly like these headers:

Date,Platform,Campaign,Adset,Ad_Name,Keyword,Match_Type,Spend,Impressions,Clicks,LPV,Conversions,Revenue,ATC

📐 Tab Summaries & Core Metrics

  • Overview & Funnels: Scans blended spends, ROAS, and CPA. Highlights click drop-offs (clicks to LPVs, and cart checkouts CVR) with neon alert indicators.
  • Daily Run Rate: Tracks daily spends, ROAS, and CPA. Projections calculate Month-to-Date spend velocity and project End-of-Month (EOM) overspends vs budget limits.
  • Google Ads Analyzer: Drills down into Google Search keyword matrices (Match type, conversions, ROAS, CPA) and Search Terms lists. Works for PMax, Shopping, and Demand Gen.
  • AI Root Cause: Allocates revenue change ($\Delta R$) to Traffic, checkout Conversion Rate (CVR), and average basket size (AOV) using mathematical attribution, visualized on a Waterfall chart.
  • Daily Intelligence: Compares the Last 3 Days against the 7-day average baseline to deliver SCALE, PAUSE, or Creative Fatigue signals based on targets.
