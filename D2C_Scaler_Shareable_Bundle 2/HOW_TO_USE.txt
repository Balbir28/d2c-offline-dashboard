========================================================================
🚀 D2C SCALER PERFORMANCE COMMAND CENTER - QUICK START GUIDE
========================================================================

Follow these 3 simple steps to open and use the Performance Dashboard:

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: ⚠️ IMPORTANT: UNZIP / EXTRACT THE BUNDLE FIRST ⚠️
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Do NOT double-click the files directly inside the ZIP folder! 
For the offline libraries and templates to work correctly:
1. Right-click the "D2C_Scaler_Shareable_Bundle.zip" file.
2. Select "Extract All" (Windows) or double-click to unzip (Mac).
3. Open the newly created unzipped folder.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: LAUNCH THE DASHBOARD
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Double-click the "index.html" file inside the folder.
2. It will instantly open in your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge).
3. The dashboard will automatically populate with 30 days of simulated 
   D2C mock data (ROAS, funnels, heatmaps, waterfall charts) so you can 
   explore the app immediately!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: HOW TO LOAD YOUR OWN DATA
------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can load your own store metrics in two ways:

👉 METHOD A: Drag-and-Drop Local Excel (100% Offline)
   1. Open the included template: "D2C_Marketing_Dashboard_Template.xlsx"
   2. Paste your Meta, Google, Sales, and Product data into the tabs.
   3. Save and close the Excel file.
   4. Open "index.html" in your browser, go to the "Data Ingestion" tab 
      (the last tab), and drag & drop the saved Excel file onto the screen.
   5. Your dashboard will refresh and load your metrics instantly!

👉 METHOD B: Google Sheets Live Sync (Online)
   1. Upload the "D2C_Marketing_Dashboard_Template.xlsx" template to Google Drive.
   2. Open it as Google Sheets.
   3. Click File -> Share -> Share with others, and change permissions to 
      "Anyone with the link can view" (Viewer access).
   4. Copy the URL of your Google Sheet.
   5. Go to the "Data Ingestion" tab of your dashboard (index.html), paste 
      the URL in the box, and click "Sync Google Sheet".
   6. The dashboard will load and sync your live metrics!
